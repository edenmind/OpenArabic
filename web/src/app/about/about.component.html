About

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

If you know some Arabic and are striving to switch from reading Islamic texts in English to Arabic; then OpenArabic is a reading platform — featuring short bilingual texts and quizzes to strengthen vocabulary — that will help you in that switch, inshāʾAllāh.

Shaykh al-Islām Ibn Taymiyah (may Allāh have mercy on him) said: "The Arabic language is the symbol of Islam and its people." (Iqtida’ al-Sirāt al-Mustaqīm, 1/519)

Islamic Foundation

The foundation of Open Arabic is based upon the Qurʼān and the Prophetic Sunnah based on the first generations of Muslims understanding with texts from authors such as Ibn Rajab al-Hanbali, Ibn Taymiyyah, Ibn Qayyim al-Jawziyya, Imam Nawawī, and many more (may Allah be pleased with them).

Open Source

The platform that Open Arabic runs upon is built using Open Source tools such as Angular, .NET, and Kubernetes. If you are interested in helping out with the development, then please check out the Open Arabic project on GitHub.
