About

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

If you know some Arabic and strive to switch from reading Islamic texts in English to read in Arabic; then OpenArabic is a reading platform — featuring short bilingual texts and vocabulary quizzes — that will help you with that switch, inshāʾAllāh.

Shaykh al-Islām Ibn Taymiyah (may Allāh have mercy on him) said: "The Arabic language is the symbol of Islam and its people." (Iqtida’ al-Sirāt al-Mustaqīm, 1/519)

Foundation

The theological foundation of OpenArabic is based upon the Qurʼān, the Prophetic Sunnah and the first generations of Muslims understanding with texts from Islamic Scholars such as Ibn Rājab al-Hanbali, Ibn Taymiyyah, Ibn Qayyim al-Jawziyya, Imām Nawawī, and many more (may Allāh be pleased with them).

Open Source

The platform that OpenArabic runs upon is built using Open Source tools such as Angular, .NET, and Kubernetes. If you are interested in helping out with the development, then please check out the Open Arabic project on GitHub.
